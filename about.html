About The Rice Wagon

The Rice Wagon is a local food truck serving Hawaiian-inspired comfort food with bold flavor and simple ingredients. Built on a passion for great food and community, The Rice Wagon brings a taste of the islands to every plate.

Our menu is inspired by traditional Hawaiian dishes, featuring favorites like kalua pork, shoyu chicken, and crispy chicken katsu. Every meal is prepared fresh and served with classic sides like rice and macaroni salad to give you a true island-style experience.

Whether you're stopping by for a quick lunch, grabbing dinner with friends, or booking us for a catering event, our goal is simple: serve quality food that people remember.

Located in Cherokee, North Carolina, The Rice Wagon travels to different locations and events, bringing great food wherever we go.
